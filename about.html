My new journey as a cafe` lover.

After several months of touring in coffee shops, I've made the big jump over to the touring in other cities' coffee shops.

How did the journey start?

My love for cafes began with a simple cup of coffee. I still remember the cozy atmosphere, the aroma of freshly ground beans, and that first sip of perfectly brewed coffee that ignited a passion within me.

From that moment on, I found myself seeking out new cafes, eager to explore their unique vibes, delicious pastries, and specialty drinks. Each visit became an opportunity to unwind, connect with friends, or dive into a good book.
